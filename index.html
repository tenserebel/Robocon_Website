About Team Volta

MIT-ADT offers many opportunities for students in different fields in the form of different clubs. Team VOLTA being one of the prominent Robotics club offers :
New learning experiences covering all the branches of engineering
Participation opportunities in Robotics competitions giving National and international exposure

Formed in March 2019 under the Mechatronics Department, Team Volta has been working consistently and has participated in Robocon 2020, 2021 and 2022.

Meet the Team

The team consists of dedicated members from various fields, led by experienced leaders. They are committed to creating exceptional projects and giving their best in every competition. With so many accomplishments already under their belts, they would be taking in new members and inspiring others in the field of robotics.
